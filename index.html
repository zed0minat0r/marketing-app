Before you join

Straight answers.

Is there a free trial?
Yes — 14 days free, no credit card required. You get full access to the Growth plan during your trial. At the end, pick a plan or walk away. Join the waitlist below to reserve your spot.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Text CANCEL at any time and your plan ends at the close of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no retention calls, no friction. We want you to stay because it's working — not because leaving is hard.
What social platforms do you support?
Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Google Business Profile. We post natively to each platform — right format, right length, right hashtags per platform. Twitter/X support coming in Q3 2026.
Is this live? When does access open?
We're in early access, opening to the first 500 businesses in May 2026. Join the waitlist and we'll text you directly when your spot is ready — no checking back, no logging in to see your status.
What happens after I sign up?
We text you within 60 seconds. You'll answer 5 short questions about your business — name, what you sell, your target customer, your biggest goal, and which social platforms you use. That's it. Your first week of posts lands in your phone within the hour.
Will I ever run out of content?
On the Starter plan, we create up to 100 pieces of content per month — roughly 4 posts per week plus review responses and a website audit. Growth gives you 500, which covers daily posting, ad variations, and more. Pro is unlimited. Edits on content we already sent don't count against your limit.
Do you actually post for me, or just draft?
Both. You choose. Reply YES to a post and we publish it automatically. Reply EDIT and we revise it. Reply SKIP and we move on. Want us to auto-post without asking? Switch to autopilot mode and we publish everything on your behalf.
What if it posts something wrong or off-brand?
Nothing posts without your YES. Every piece of content is sent to you first. If it's off, reply EDIT and say what to change — we revise and send it back. After a few rounds, we learn your voice and get it right more often. You're always in control.
